Room-mate Finder

A webapp to find your room-mate and wing-mates at IIT Bombay
More Info




About

This app gets the information from the users itself (You!). If you don't find any roomate or wingmate at first, don't be disappointed. Instead, be sure to fill up your own information, and then check back up later. Hopefully, most of the students will fill it up and you can find your mates! Do spread the word about the app, because the more people who use it, the better chances of finding your roommate.
